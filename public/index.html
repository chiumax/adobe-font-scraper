Adobe Font Download Utility

Downloads .ttf font files from any Adobe font link.

This app is in no way affiliated with Adobe Inc. or any of their products.

Font Families with many fonts take longer to download.

URLs with "collections" in them are not supported.

This is built with express.js and uses URLs loaded into the browser from Adobe Fonts.
