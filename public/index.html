Adobe Font Download Utility
Downloads .ttf font files from any Adobe font link.
This app is in no way affiliated with Adobe Inc. or any of their products. Disclaimer! If you are looking to download a URL with 'collections' in it, the app does not recursively scrape each font family. Also, quite frankly, it is pretty bugged... will not give straight results This is build with express.js and uses URLs loaded into the browser from Adobe Fonts.
